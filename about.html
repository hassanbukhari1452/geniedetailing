About Genie Detailing

Passionate about perfection, dedicated to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Genie Detailing began with a simple mission: to provide unparalleled mobile auto detailing services that exceed our clients' expectations. What started as a passion project has grown into the region's most trusted premium detailing service.

Our journey began when frustrated with the lack of quality mobile detailing options, I decided to create the service he wished existed. Armed with professional-grade equipment and an unwavering commitment to excellence, I started serving clients one vehicle at a time.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers throughout the metro area, maintaining the same attention to detail and personal touch that defined our early days.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional mobile auto detailing services that restore and protect our clients' vehicles while providing unmatched convenience and customer satisfaction. We believe every vehicle deserves professional care, and every client deserves to feel valued.

Our Vision

To be the leading mobile auto detailing service in the region, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer service. We envision a future where premium car care is accessible, convenient, and environmentally responsible.

Our Values

Excellence

We pursue perfection in every detail, using only the finest products and techniques to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Integrity

Honest communication, transparent pricing, and ethical business practices form the foundation of every client relationship.

Innovation

We continuously adopt the latest techniques and technologies to provide cutting-edge detailing solutions.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility guides our choice of products and processes, ensuring minimal impact on our planet.
