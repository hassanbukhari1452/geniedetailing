Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be present during the service?

While not required, we recommend being available at the start and end of service. We can work while you're at home or office.

What if it rains on my scheduled day?

We monitor weather conditions and will contact you to reschedule if necessary. Your satisfaction and safety are our priorities.

Do you provide water and electricity?

We bring our own water supply. We only need access to a standard electrical outlet for our equipment.

How long does a typical service take?

Service time varies by package: Interior detail (2-3 hours), Exterior correction (3-4 hours), Complete detail (4-6 hours).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Do you offer package deals or loyalty discounts?

Yes! We offer package deals for multiple services and loyalty discounts for repeat customers. Ask about our maintenance plans.
